<h2><strong>Let's build Marsiling together-your opinion counts!</strong></h2>
<hr>
<p>We believe residents should have a direct say in shaping our community.
Marsiling offers multiple opportunities for your voice to be heard:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Voting for Amenities</strong>:
<br>Choose your preferred facilities, like those at Marsiling Cascadia—making
your neighbourhood truly yours!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>House Visits:</strong>
<br>Regular visits to hear your feedback directly, ensuring your ideas shape
Marsiling’s future.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Makan Kakis:</strong>
<br>Friendly sessions to bond with neighbours over meals, creating stronger,
closer ties within our community.</p>
</li>
</ul>
