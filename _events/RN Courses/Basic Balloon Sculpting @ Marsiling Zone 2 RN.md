<h3><strong>Course Overview</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/RN Courses 2024/balloon_craft_9.jpg">
</div>
<p>
<br>Balloon Sculpting has been getting more popular in Singapore these days.
It is a must-have for event give away &amp; children's party, balloons
are being sculpted into different shapes and sizes such as dogs, bears,
flowers, heart, even cartoon characters and other customized sculptures.
It is a wonderful souvenir to bring home. Balloons are commonly said to
be a child's best friend.
<br>
<br>The basic balloon sculpting class is catered for those who have no twisting
experience but interested to explore the fun of balloon sculpting &amp;
feel achievement with their end products. There will be 2 basic sculptures
to bring home by end of each lesson.</p>
<h3><strong>Requirements and Remarks</strong></h3>
<p>Things for participants to bring for lesson as follows:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Scissor</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Black marker</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plastic bag to keep the sculptures</p>
</li>
</ol>

<h4><strong>Training Provider(s)</strong></h4>
<hr>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="max-width: 100%; vertical-align: middle; width: 135px; height: auto;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="_" src="https://www.onepa.gov.sg/-/media/Images/TrainingProviders/Wong-Wei-Kang-Edwin-480BE92D0098E911811600155D464800.png?h=144&amp;w=144&amp;rev=5bb58f450a024a23afcbf220ab7880ed&amp;hash=33FA79F66453452D2A06EC5250574336">
</div>
<p><strong>Wong Wei Kang, Edwin</strong>
</p>
<p>Edwin Wong, an experienced professional with over 10 years in the kids'
entertainment industry. Edwin's passion lies in the art of balloon sculpting
and magic tricks and aiming to bring joy to his audiences of all ages.
Edwin's strength lies not only in his skills but also in his ability to
connect with people. His friendly and approachable nature enables him to
build strong relationships with participants, be it children or adults.
<br>
<br>Edwin has worked with organizations and schools like Gardens by the Bay,
Ngee Ann Polytechnic, CHIJ St Nicholas School, Raffles Institution, Housing
Development Board, and others.
<br>His true passion lies in sharing the world of magic and balloon sculpting
with anyone eager to explore the art together.</p>
