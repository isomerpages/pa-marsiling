<h2><strong>Celebrating &amp; Supporting Marsiling Students: Brighter Futures Ahead</strong></h2>
<blockquote>
<p>Marsiling remains dedicated to empowering our students, ensuring they
have the support needed for a successful and fulfilling educational journey.</p>
</blockquote>
<hr>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Edusave Awards:</strong>
</p>
<p>Recognises <strong>1,400 Marsiling students annually</strong> for outstanding
academic and non-academic achievements.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Marsiling Cares Student Grant:</strong>
</p>
<p>Provides <strong>bursaries to over 1,600 students annually</strong>, significantly
expanding financial support from the previous 200 students under the former
Sembcorp Marine scheme.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Ready for School Initiative:</strong>
</p>
<p>Supports <strong>120 pre-schoolers annually</strong>, preparing our youngest
residents for a confident start in education.</p>
</li>
</ul>
