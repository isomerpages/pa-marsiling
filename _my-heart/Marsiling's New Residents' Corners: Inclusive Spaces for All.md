<h2><strong>Marsiling's New Residents' Corners: Inclusive Spaces for All</strong></h2>
<blockquote>
<p>Marsiling will soon welcome two <strong>New Concept Residents' Corners</strong> at <strong>Blk 16 and Blk 160</strong>,
thoughtfully designed by residents in collaboration with grassroots leaders
and Nanyang Polytechnic</p>

<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Inclusive by Design:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Welcoming spaces for residents of all ages and abilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Accessible for seniors and wheelchair users (<strong>Blk 16</strong>).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conveniently located near Marsiling MRT (<strong>Blk 160</strong>) for
easy community access.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Building Community Bonds:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Perfect hubs for hobby groups, educational workshops, and health screenings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promote intergenerational bonding and reduce social isolation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Supporting Health &amp; Well-being:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Encourage active lifestyles and regular social interactions, benefiting
physical and mental health, especially for seniors.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Connecting the Community:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Serve as central information points for community news and initiatives.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Facilitate knowledge-sharing, skills exchange, and lifelong learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
