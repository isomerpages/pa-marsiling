<h2><strong>Supporting Your Health Journey: Marsiling’sCommunity Physical Activity Programmes (CPAP)</strong></h2>
<hr>
<p><strong>Marsiling’s</strong>  <strong>Community Physical Activity Programmes (CPAP)</strong> have
inspired over 2,000 residents each year to enjoy active, healthier lifestyles
since launching in 2018.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Diverse and inclusive activities for all ages:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Zumba, yoga, and pilates</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cardio, stretching, and strength training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wellness programs specifically tailored for seniors</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Conveniently located in Marsiling neighbourhood spaces, accessible to
everyone.</p>
</li>
</ul>
