<h2><strong>Expanding Pre School Options : So Every child has a Bright Start</strong></h2>
<hr>
<p>Parents in Marsiling can now choose from a variety of modern pre-school
options that nurture early learning and growth. These established centres,
featuring state-of-the-art facilities and enriching programs, are dedicated
to offering positive, early learning experiences.</p>
<p><strong>Proven &amp; Delivered:</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Launched in May 2017, one centre has enrolled over 740 pre-schoolers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Six other PCF centres welcome around 120 new enrolments annually.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our Pre-School Providers:</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>PCF Mega Childcare Centre (near Woodlands Stadium)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PCF Blk 32 Childcare &amp; Kindergarten Centre</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PCF Blk 12 Childcare Centre</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>NTUC My First Skool, Blk 187 Marsiling Grove</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Skool4Kidz Preschool @ Marsiling Drive</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>M.Y World @ Marsiling Greenview</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Additional options include 2 centres in Zone 1 (one next to Skool4Kidz)
and the YMCA in Zone 5</p>
</li>
</ul>
